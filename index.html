My journey began in high school, where I explored Design and Visual Communication, learning graphic communication and 3D modeling. Programming, where I gained skills in HTML/CSS and JavaScript. And Robotics, focusing on C++ embedded programming and CAD software.

I was captivated by the process of bringing ideas to life through technology, which inspired me to pursue Computer Science at the University of Auckland to deepen my understanding and expertise.

Currently, I use Figma and React.js for front-end development and Node.js, Express.js, and MongoDB for the back end. I’m expanding my skills with .NET Core, PostgreSQL, and Microsoft SQL to strengthen my full- stack capabilities.

I also work with Bash, Docker, Selenium, and Jenkins for DevOps, and I’m exploring PyTorch for AI. My end goal is to apply my Full Stack and DevOps experience with cloud platforms like AWS and Azure for efficient testing and deployment.
